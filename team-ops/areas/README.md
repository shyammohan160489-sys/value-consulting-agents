# Areas

**Ongoing responsibilities with no end date.**

The difference between an Area and an Engagement: an Engagement closes; an Area is permanent.

## Examples of areas a Value Consulting team lead might track

- **Team health** — capacity, morale, retention signals
- **Hiring** — open roles, pipeline, interview load
- **Capability building** — skill gaps, training plans, coaching investments
- **Ops cadence** — weekly / monthly rituals, their effectiveness
- **Methodology evolution** — how the team's playbook is changing
- **Cross-functional relationships** — health of partnerships with AEs, SEs, delivery
- **Personal leadership growth** — your own development as a manager

## Cadence

Each area page declares its own review cadence (weekly / biweekly / monthly / quarterly) in front-matter. The weekly review skill will surface areas due for review.

## File naming

`<area-slug>.md` — e.g., `team-health.md`, `hiring.md`, `capability-building.md`.
