# Backbase Add-Ons & Premium Modules

## Identity
- **Model:** Flat annual fee or per-authentication
- **Range:** $50K – $200K/year
- **Bundling:** Often paired with Digital Banking for security story

## Banking Premium
- **Model:** Per user uplift on base Digital Banking rate
- **Range:** +$0.50 – $2.00/user/year on top of base rate
- **Features:** Advanced analytics, AI-powered insights, premium UX

## Conversational Banking
- **Model:** Flat annual fee + per-interaction overage
- **Range:** $75K – $250K/year base
- **Note:** Separate pricing line from Digital Banking

## AI / Intelligence
- **Model:** Flat annual fee or per-user uplift
- **Range:** $50K – $300K/year depending on scope
- **Features:** AI-powered recommendations, predictive analytics, NLP

## Key Commercial Considerations
- Add-ons are margin-accretive — higher ARPU with limited cost-to-serve increase
- Bundle 2-3 add-ons for "Premium" tier positioning
- Cross-sell add-ons at renewal to justify ARR uplift
- Identity + Conversational are popular cross-sell targets
