# Digital Banking Platform

## Licensing Model
- **Primary metric:** Per active user per year
- **Alternative:** Block-based (pool of users at a fixed block price)
- **Typical block sizes:** 50K, 100K, 250K, 500K users

## Pricing Reference Ranges
- **Retail (emerging markets):** $1.00 – $3.00 per user/year
- **Retail (developed markets):** $2.00 – $6.00 per user/year
- **SME/Business banking:** $5.00 – $15.00 per user/year (lower volume, higher value)

## Common Bundling Patterns
- Digital Banking + Onboarding: Often bundled as "Digital Channel Platform"
- Digital Banking + Identity: Security + channel bundle
- Digital Banking + Assist: Full front-to-back customer experience

## Hosting Considerations
- **Managed hosting:** Included in platform fee or separate line item
- **Client-hosted:** Lower Backbase cost-to-serve, potentially lower pricing
- **Hybrid:** Mixed model, pricing depends on split

## Key Commercial Considerations
- Block pricing reduces true-up friction (no per-user audit)
- Per-user pricing aligns cost to value (more users = more revenue for client)
- High-volume clients (1M+ users) get significant ARPU compression
- Low-ARPU segments risk margin erosion at scale — protect with floors
