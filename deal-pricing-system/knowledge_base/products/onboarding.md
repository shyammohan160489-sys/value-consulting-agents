# Backbase Onboarding

## Licensing Model
- **Typically bundled** with Digital Banking or Lending
- **Standalone:** Per application/onboarding volume or flat annual fee
- Rarely priced as a separate per-user line item

## Pricing Reference Ranges
- **Bundled:** Included in Digital Banking rate (adds $0.50-$1.50/user to bundle)
- **Standalone (flat):** $75K – $300K/year depending on scope
- **Per application:** $3 – $15 per completed onboarding

## Common Bundling Patterns
- Most common: Bundled into Digital Banking deal as "Digital Channel + Onboarding"
- Lending + Onboarding: Loan origination use case
- Business Banking + Onboarding: SME account opening

## Key Commercial Considerations
- Bundling simplifies pricing and avoids line-item negotiation
- Standalone onboarding is harder to sell without a channel play
- Value story: reduced onboarding time, higher conversion rates, lower drop-off
