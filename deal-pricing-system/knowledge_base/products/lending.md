# Backbase Lending

## Licensing Model
- **Primary metric:** AUM-based (basis points on loan portfolio)
- **Alternative:** Per application/origination volume
- **Alternative:** Flat annual fee for smaller portfolios

## Pricing Reference Ranges
- **AUM-based:** 0.5 – 3.0 basis points on total loan portfolio
- **Per application:** $5 – $25 per application (high volume origination)
- **Flat fee:** $100K – $500K/year for smaller/focused implementations

## Common Bundling Patterns
- Lending + Onboarding: End-to-end loan origination journey
- Lending + Digital Banking: Full retail channel play
- Often phased: Digital Banking first, Lending Year 2-3

## Key Commercial Considerations
- AUM-based aligns revenue to portfolio growth — attractive for growth banks
- Basis points feel "free" to bankers compared to large license fees
- Start with flat fee, convert to AUM when portfolio scales
- Seasonal origination patterns may make per-application volatile
