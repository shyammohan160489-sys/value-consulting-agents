The Trade-Off
Why Scenario 2 aligns cost with implementation reality

Scenario 1: Original Quote

  • ✗ $1.4M in Y1 while system is still under implementation — paying full price before full value
  • ✓ No LOB commitment caveat — Y3-Y5 pricing is unconditional
  • ✓ Lower Y3-Y5 at $1.74M/yr — flatter long-term cost
  • ✗ Board approval harder — justifying $1.4M upfront during build phase
Annual Cost Pattern
$1.40M → $1.40M → $1.74M → $1.74M → $1.74M

Scenario 2: Implementation-Aligned

  • ✓ Y1 at $800K — 43% lower, aligned with wealth-only build phase
  • ✓ Y2 at $1.05M — still 25% below original as wealth goes live
  • ✓ $950K more net value in Y1-Y2 — proves ROI before full commitment
  • ⚠ Y3 step-up to $2.06M — reflects scope tripling (1 LOB → 3 LOBs)
Annual Cost Pattern
$0.80M → $1.05M → $2.06M → $2.06M → $2.06M

The logic:   Y1-Y2 = you pay for what's live (Wealth only).   Y3-Y5 = you pay for the full platform (Wealth + Retail + Business).   The Y3 step-up is not a price increase on the same scope — it reflects the activation of two additional lines of business.
