What matters more: platform breadth or lending capability?

Recommended Negotiation Position
How to respond if HNB asks for Lending
Primary Position

Keep Retail, Phase Lending

  • 1 "Retail-to-wealth conversion is your #1 value driver at $10-15M." With Retail on Backbase, those conversions happen at retail rates. Without it, each convert costs more on the wealth tier.
  • 2 "Lending is a natural Phase 2." Once Wealth is live (Y2), we scope Lending with its own validated business case and incremental budget.
  • 3 "1.88M users on one platform = better lending decisions later." Retail transaction data improves credit scoring when Lending is added in Phase 2.

Give-to-get: Complimentary Lending Discovery Workshop (2-3 days) as part of the deal.

Fallback Position

Offer the Scope Trade at $8.75M

  • 1 Present both options. Be transparent about the $750K premium from wealth migration fees.
  • 2 Show the benefits impact: validated $20-25M vs mixed $15-29M range.
  • 3 Recommend a lending mini-assessment (4-6 weeks) to quantify lending benefits before committing.
Best Outcome

Both: Option A + Lending Expansion

HNB takes Option A ($8M) + adds Lending as Y2-Y3 upsell (~$300-400K/yr). Total deal: $9.5-10M. Both capabilities. Both business cases validated. No compromises.
