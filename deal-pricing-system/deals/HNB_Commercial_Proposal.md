## Executive Summary

Hatton National Bank stands at a strategic inflection point. With **2.8 million customers** but only **13,000 wealth management relationships**, HNB has a $10-15M revenue opportunity by converting just 5-10% of its customer base into active wealth clients. However, today's fragmented digital landscape—multiple systems, manual processes, and siloed data—is preventing HNB from capturing this opportunity.

**The Challenge:**
The original pricing proposal of **$1.4M in Year 1** has created a roadblock.

**Our Solution:**
We've restructured the commercial proposal to balance **Implementation phases and timelines** with **long-term value realization**. The revised pricing features a **phased value ramp** aligned to launch milestones—a significantly lower entry during the Wealth pilot phase, scaling with Retail/SME activation. This structure delivers:

- **Y1 starting point:** $0.80M (43% below original quote)
- **Phased growth:** Lower entry in Y1-Y2, then scaling with platform launches
- **Launch alignment:** Pricing scales from 130K Wealth users (Y1-Y2) to 1.88M total users (Y3-Y5)
- **Total 5-year investment:** $8.0M
- **Expected 5-year benefit:** $15-35M in revenue uplift and cost avoidance

**Payback:** 14-18 months through retail-to-wealth upgrades, digital activation, and RM capacity redeployment.

## Part 5: Risk Mitigation & Protections

### 5.1 Launch Delay Protection

**Concern:** "What if Retail/SME launch delays beyond Y3?"

**Protection Clause:**
Y3 pricing activates ONLY upon:
- Retail/SME launch, OR
- Achievement of 500K active users

Whichever comes first. If launch delays to Y4, rates remain at Y2 level until trigger is met, then adjust the following quarter.

### 5.2 Adoption Risk Mitigation

**Minimum User Floor:** None in Years 1-2 (proof-of-value phase)

**Rationale:** We share adoption risk during Wealth pilot. HNB commits to launch plan, Backbase commits to full implementation support regardless of early adoption pace.

### 5.3 Benchmark Comparison

**What Others Pay (Blended ARPU for similar deployments):**
- Standard Backbase pricing: $5-6 per user per year
- HNB Y3-Y5 pricing: **$0.84-$1.07 per user** (85% below standard)
- HNB already receiving volume economics in base pricing

### 5.4 Performance Incentives

**Overperformance Reward:**
If HNB exceeds 2.2M active users by Y4 (15% above plan):
- Y5 rates reduced by 10% ($200K annual savings)
- Reduced rate extended through Y6-Y7
- Upside sharing vs pre-discounting planned volume
