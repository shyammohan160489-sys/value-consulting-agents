1.2 Current State: The Challenges

Digital Fragmentation

  • Multiple frontline apps (HNB Fit, DL mobile, Lime) with disconnected UX
  • Fragile integration mesh between frontend and core systems
  • Slow time-to-market for new features
  • High vendor dependency—HNB lacks direct control of digital roadmap

Manual Operating Model

  • Paper-heavy onboarding and servicing for non-retail segments
  • RM time lost to admin instead of client relationships
  • Portfolio reviews require 1-2 hours of manual data consolidation
  • Change capacity tied to "keeping the lights on"

Untapped Opportunity

  • 2.8M total customers, only 13K WM clients (0.5% penetration)
  • 30% of Sri Lankan population (1.8M households) are Emerging Affluent but don't consider HNB's WM proposition
  • Competitors with holistic digital propositions are capturing wallet share
  • Average Sri Lankan has 3-4 financial relationships—HNB is losing to NDB Wealth, Capital Alliance, JB Advantage
