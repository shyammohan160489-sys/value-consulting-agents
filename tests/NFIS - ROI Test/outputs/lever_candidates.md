## Problem Statement

**(a) Bank's desired outcome:** Navy Federal Investment Services wants to grow from 115K to 800K Digital Investor (DI) members by 2030 while raising member satisfaction from 58% to 75%, reducing asset outflow to competitors (Schwab, Fidelity, Robinhood, Vanguard), and elevating NFIS from a standalone "island" product into a seamlessly integrated investing experience for 14M+ NFCU members. In their words: "the preferred and trusted digital financial advisor for our members' financial well-being" (E52).

**(b) Backbase's sales objective:** Position the Backbase Digital Invest platform as the transformation engine to move NFIS from an affiliated investment provider to a primary investing destination — integrating DI into the NFCU banking experience, automating the onboarding-to-funding journey, enabling multi-channel engagement, and creating automated tier-transition paths from self-directed to advisor-managed.

**(c) Primary problem type:** Type 6: AUM-Based / Advisory Business. Secondary: Type 3 (Digital Transformation / Channel Adoption), Type 2 (Cost Reduction / Cost to Serve).

**(d) Scope constraints:** USA only. NFIS is a division of NFCU (credit union, 14M+ members). Focus on Digital Investor (DI) and Financial Advisor (FA) tiers. Self-directed and robo-advisory products in scope. Credit union's member-first pricing philosophy constrains fee-based revenue levers (E43). Custodian migration to Apex is an upcoming dependency (E81). Branch support for DI is out of scope currently (E106).

A propensity-to-invest (P2I) model exists but is not connected to DI — only linked to marketing (E33).

**Operational Change:** Backbase Digital Engage delivers next-best-action (NBA) prompts within the NFCU banking app: life-event triggers (salary increase, bonus deposit, surplus savings), embedded investment entry points visible on the banking dashboard, and connection of the P2I model to real-time DI account opening flows. Backbase Digital Banking integration surfaces DI product visibility (balance summary, quick-links) directly within the main banking experience, eliminating the "island" architecture (E54, E28, E34). Digital Onboarding pre-populates DI account opening from existing NFCU banking profile — no re-KYC required.

**Volume/Rate Impact:** Current DI penetration: ~0.8% (115K/14M). Credit union benchmark: 3-5% (E9). Gap: ~2-4pp. Conservative Backbase capture rate: 40%. Target improvement: +1-2pp = 140K-280K new DI accounts over the model period. Derivation: gap-based method.

**Backbase Enabler:** Digital Engage (NBA engine), Digital Banking (embedded investment visibility), Digital Onboarding (pre-populated account opening), Data Foundations (P2I model integration)

**Confidence: HIGH** — Root driver directly evidenced (E1, E8, E9, E11, E56, E57, E59), benchmark gap is large and well-documented, Backbase cross-sell capability is proven in investing domain (roi_levers.md Lever 1).
